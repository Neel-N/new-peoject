PLMS Orchestra "Gives Bach" to Elementary Students

Posted by Devin Felix on 28 May, 2019

On May 15, 74 members of the Pine Lake Middle School seventh grade orchestra visited Thorndyke Elementary in Tukwila to share a music program called “Giving Bach” with second- through fifth-grade students. Pine Lake students became teachers and worked one-on-one with elementary students, giving a hands-on musical experience with a string instrument. In a matter of minutes, the elementary students were playing along to a song. Pine Lake students enjoyed performing, teaching, leading games, and participating in recess with the students of Thorndyke Elementary.
